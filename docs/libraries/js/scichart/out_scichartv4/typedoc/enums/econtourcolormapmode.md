### AlternateColors

AlternateColors: = "AlternateColors"

Applies the Gradient Colors from [UniformContoursRenderableSeries](https://www.scichart.com/documentation/js/v4/typedoc/classes/uniformcontoursrenderableseries.html) ColorMap individually, ignoring offsets, e.g. with Gradient Stops Red, Green, Blue then contour lines will appear Red Green or Blue

### GradientColors

GradientColors: = "GradientColors"

Applies the Gradient Colors from [UniformContoursRenderableSeries](https://www.scichart.com/documentation/js/v4/typedoc/classes/uniformcontoursrenderableseries.html) ColorMap according to the data on the chart
